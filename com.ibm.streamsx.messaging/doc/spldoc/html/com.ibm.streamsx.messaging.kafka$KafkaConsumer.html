com.ibm.streamsx.messaging.kafka::KafkaConsumer

com.ibm.streamsx.messaging -> com.ibm.streamsx.messaging.kafka-> KafkaConsumer

This operator acts as a Kafka consumer recieving messages for one or more topics. Note that there may be multiple threads receiving messages depending on the configuration specified. Ordering of messages is not guaranteed.
